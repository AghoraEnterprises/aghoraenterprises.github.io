About Us

Why We Are

Our vision and hope in contributing to a sustainable planet, leading us on our path of environmental management for future generations.

What We Do

We create value by bringing proper solutions to those generating waste and to consumers who want recyclable materials.

Why Choose Us

We endeavor to eliminate unhealthy impacts by directing materials for highest and best utilization to an equally minded environmentally conscious manufacturing sector.

Our Services

Assessment and Advising

Our trained professionals will survey your site and advise the best solution & value for your assets. We also provide specialized assessment for power, steel, chemical plant 7 machinery.

Dismantling Services

We have been dealing plant & machinery for few years. We recently started offering complete dismantling services for plant & machinery that were locked out. Our experience will get you values as high as possible.

Mortgage Clearance

We can help you to clear the mortgage or bank loans and can offer end-to-end liquidating solution for plant & machinery of any size for customers. Please let us know the machineries & location required and we will discuss which option is right for your needs.

Consulting

Our guidance and hands on approach ensures you are receiving the best waste management possible. We brand your materials in mills and assure continued movement with favorable pricing.
